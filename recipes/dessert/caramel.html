Caramel

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup dark Karo corn syrup
  • 1 cube (1/2 cup) butter
  • ½ pint cream
  • ½ pint cream
  1. Rub pan and wood spoon with Nucoa margarine.
  2. Cook sugar, corn syrup, butter, and half the cream at medium high heat to 224° (pecan roll) or 226º (caramel).
  3. Add the other half of the cream and cook to 228° (pecan roll) or 230º (caramel).
  4. Pour into buttered 9x13 pan—don’t scrape the pan!

(Temperatures adjusted for 4000 feet altitude).
