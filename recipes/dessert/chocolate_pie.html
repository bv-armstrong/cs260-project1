Chocolate Pie

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • pinch salt
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • 6 ounces dark chocolate
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • Chocolate crumb pie crust
  1. In medium heavy saucepan, blend first 4 ingredients.
  2. Add egg yolks, whisk to form thick paste.
  3. Whisk in milk and cream. Continue whisking while heating over medium heat until mixture thickens and boils for 1 minute.
  4. Remove from heat. Add chocolate, mix until smooth.
  5. Add vanilla.
  6. Let cool for 5 minutes, whisking occasionally.
  7. Put in crust, chill until cold.
