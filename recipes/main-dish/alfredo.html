Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, cubed
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt to taste
  • 8 ounces cooked, drained fettuccine
  1. Melt cream cheese, parmesan cheese milk, butter, white pepper, garlic powder, and salt in medium sauce pan.
  2. Cook on low until cream cheese is melted and mixture is well blended.
  3. Pour over fettuccine.
